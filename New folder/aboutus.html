KINEMATICS The portion describing motion is called kinematics. DYNAMICS The portion which explains motion is called dynamics. Note; Force is a fundamental concept in dynamics Force can change the velocity of object. Force does not always cause motion. Force is commonly understood as a push or a pull. Force is defined precisely by Newton’s laws of motion. FORCE(F ⃗) Force acting on a body is defined as the product of the mass of the body and its acceleration.
